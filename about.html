Who we are

Our distinct global network of people, data and relationships creates a mindset that allows Wintrust Financial Corporation to spot opportunities, manage risks and connect dots for our clients in ways that others simply cannot.


Our Mission

  1. To serve as a trusted partner to our clients by responsibly providing financial services that enable growth and economic progress.
  2. We protect people's savings and help them make the purchases – from everyday transactions to buying a home – that improve the quality of their lives.
  3. We work with companies to optimize their daily operations, whether they need working capital, to make payroll or export their goods overseas.
  4. We strive to earn and maintain the public's trust by constantly adhering to the highest ethical standards.
